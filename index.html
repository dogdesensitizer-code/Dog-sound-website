How to Use Calm Dog Thunder

  1. Choose a session length (5–30 minutes).
  2. Set master volume low and intensity to 1–2.
  3. Enable "Soften low rumble" for sensitive dogs.
  4. Tap Start Session and reward calm behavior.
  5. Increase intensity slightly across sessions if your dog remains relaxed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this help my dog's thunderstorm anxiety?

Many owners find gradual sound exposure helpful. Start at low volume and short sessions, and consult your veterinarian for personalized guidance.

Is this safe for sensitive dogs?

Yes—use low volume, enable the "Soften low rumble" option, and keep sessions short. Stop if your dog shows stress.

Do I need special speakers?

No, but clear speakers help. Avoid very loud subwoofers. Keep volume comfortable.

Does this replace professional training?

No. It is an educational tool, not a substitute for veterinary or professional training advice.
