How to Use Calm Dog Thunder

  1. Choose a session length (5–30 minutes).
  2. Set master volume low (10–25%).
  3. Use Low and High frequency sliders to reduce rumbles and cracks; start around 75% reduction and ease back over time.
  4. Tap Start Session and reward calm behavior.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this help my dog's thunderstorm anxiety?

Many owners find gradual sound exposure helpful. Start at low volume and short sessions, and consult your veterinarian for personalized guidance.

What do the frequency sliders do?

Thunder has deep low-frequency rumbles and sharp high-frequency cracks. These sliders let you reduce each range independently to match your dog’s sensitivity.

Quick Setup for Best Results

  1. Use proper speakers. Thunder lives in deep lows (20–80 Hz) and bright highs (2–8 kHz).
  2. Start gentle. Keep volume low and increase slowly only if your dog stays relaxed.
  3. Keep sessions short. End on a calm note, reward, and take breaks.
