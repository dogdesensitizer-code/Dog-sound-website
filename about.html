About Calm Dog Thunder

Calm Dog Thunder was created to help dogs and their families reduce stress during thunderstorms, fireworks, and other loud events. Our site provides gentle, customizable soundscapes designed to help desensitize pets in a safe and supportive way.

Our Mission

We aim to make life easier for pet owners by offering a simple, accessible tool to calm dogs with sound therapy. Our approach combines expert knowledge and real-world testing with a focus on animal well-being.

Who We Are

Calm Dog Thunder is a small independent project built by pet lovers who understand the challenges of noise anxiety. This is not a large corporation — just people who want to help other pet owners find a practical solution.
