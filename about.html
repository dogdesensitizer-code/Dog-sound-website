About Calm Dog Thunder

Calm Dog Thunder was created to help dogs and their owners work through thunderstorm anxiety using safe, controlled sound training.

Our Mission

Many dogs experience stress during thunderstorms, fireworks, or other loud events. Our goal is to provide an accessible, easy-to-use tool that allows pet owners to gradually desensitize their dogs to these sounds, improving quality of life for both pets and their humans.

How It Works

  • Choose a training session length and intensity
  • Play realistic thunder sounds with optional rain background
  • Manually trigger thunder at chosen moments for interactive training
  • Adjust settings over time to help your dog adapt

Who We Are

We are a small, pet-focused project run by dog owners, trainers, and tech enthusiasts. Our goal is to combine love for animals with practical technology that makes a difference.
